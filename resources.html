Helpful Resources

Your guide to the most essential information and tools for a successful transition to life in the UAE.

Getting Started in the UAE

Moving to a new country can be challenging, but having the right resources at your disposal makes the transition smoother. Below, we provide a selection of resources that cover everything from legal requirements to social adaptation, job searches, and health services in the UAE.

Legal Information & Government Resources

Understanding the legal framework of the UAE is essential for immigrants. Here are some resources to help you navigate residency permits, visas, and more:

  • UAE Government Portal - Official government information and services.
  • Ministry of Human Resources & Emiratisation - Guide on labor laws and worker rights.
  • Federal Authority for Identity and Citizenship - Information on visa applications, renewals, and cancellations.

Health and Medical Resources

Having access to health services is crucial. Here are some resources to help you understand the healthcare options in the UAE:

  • Department of Health - Abu Dhabi - Information on healthcare facilities and insurance options.
  • Dubai Health Authority - Healthcare services and resources in Dubai.
  • SEHA - Emirates Health Services - Network of public hospitals and clinics in the UAE.

Employment Resources

Looking for a job in the UAE? Here are some resources that can help you find job openings, prepare for interviews, and understand employment laws:

  • Bayt - A popular job portal in the Middle East.
  • LinkedIn - Networking and job search platform widely used in the UAE.
  • GulfTalent - Job board for roles across the Gulf region.
  • Ministry of Labour Laws - Information on workers' rights and UAE labor laws.

Housing Resources

Finding a place to live can be a challenge in a new country. Here are resources to help you find rental options and understand tenant rights in the UAE:

  • Property Finder - Popular property portal for rentals and sales.
  • Dubizzle - Listings for apartments, villas, and other rental options.
  • Dubai Land Department - Information on property laws and tenant rights in Dubai.

Cultural Resources

Learning about UAE culture can make adaptation easier and more enjoyable. Here are some resources to help you understand local customs, languages, and social norms:

  • Visit Dubai - Insight into local attractions, culture, and events in Dubai.
  • Abu Dhabi Culture - Cultural information and events in Abu Dhabi.
  • Expat Woman - A resource for expats to connect, learn, and integrate into UAE society.

Education Resources

If you're moving with family or planning to continue your studies, here are some educational resources for schools, universities, and other learning opportunities:

  • Knowledge and Human Development Authority - Information on Dubai schools, universities, and education policies.
  • Ministry of Education UAE - UAE education system guidelines and school listings.
  • Edarabia - Resource for schools, universities, and education options in the UAE.

Transportation Resources

Getting around in the UAE is easy with these resources for public transportation, driving, and licensing:

  • Dubai Roads and Transport Authority - Information on metro, buses, and public transit in Dubai.
  • Department of Transport - Abu Dhabi - Transit information for Abu Dhabi.
  • Ministry of Interior - Guide on obtaining a UAE driving license.
